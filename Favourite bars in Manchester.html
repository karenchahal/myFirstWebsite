Favourite bars in Manchester

The Washhouse

This quirky hidden bar is my favourite cocktail hotspot in Manchester! It is so well disguised as laundrettte, that we walked straight past it on our first visit.
When you first arrive, you are welcomed to a washing machine door and must ask use the given telephone, to ask for a "rinse". Then you enter the cosy bar in which bartenders will come to your table and talk you through their large menu.

Below are a few pictures from my visit with some of the cockatils shown.

The Fitzgerald

Another quirky "hidden gem" in the Northern Quarter of Manchester. The Fitzgerald is a speakeasy drinking-den with a lot of Great Gatsby vibes.
The bar has some interesting drinks, as well as live jazz music on certain days and projetions of Laurel and Hardy.

Cloud 23

Situated on the 23rd floor of Beetham Tower, which was the tallest building in Manchester until 2018, there is an elegant cocktail and champagne bar called Cloud 23.
The views as you can imagine are stunning so it is best to go when it is not raining - hard in Manchester!
